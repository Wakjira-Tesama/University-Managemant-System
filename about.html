About US

ODA Special Boarding School

ODA Boarding school is an institution where children live within premises while being given formal instruction. Students in the boarding school study and live during the school year with their fellow students. The concept of boarding school is an intensive form of education in which students live at school and visit their families only at summer. The history of boarding school goes back over hundreds of years. However in the past, boarding schools were exclusively joined by monasteries and royal families. But now boarding schools are becoming popular even in developing countries and have been used to increase the educational opportunities of marginalized and disadvantaged students as well.

ODA Boarding school is opened at East Showa of Adama and Bishoftu town giving full package services besides providing with quality education. Adama Boarding school started in Adama town in February 2014G.C/2006 E.C. Since then it has been developed to be a successful boarding school proving a first class education to students from across Oromia. Both schools have been working to assure quality of education since its establishment. The teachers are supporting the students even after school time without additional payment.

Know More about Us

ODA Boarding School provides a variety of academic and extracurricular opportunities. The institution offers programs that emphasize science, technology, leadership, and talent development. With a focus on excellence, the school ensures that students not only achieve top national examination results but also acquire lifelong skills in character, innovation, and community service. Currently, ODA has established strong programs in secondary education, boarding facilities, talent development, and research-driven learning practices.

History

1990s: The Oromia Development Association (ODA) began constructing schools across Oromia to expand access to quality education for all communities. 2014 (2006 E.C): ODA opened its first Boarding School in Adama Town, East Showa Zone, marking a major step in nurturing future leaders and professionals. Following Years: The school quickly grew into a model institution providing a full package of educational and boarding services. Later Expansion: A second ODA Boarding School was established in Bishoftu Town, further extending the opportunity to more high-achieving students across the region. Present Day: Both Adama and Bishoftu ODA Boarding Schools continue to deliver first-class education, emphasizing academic excellence, moral integrity, and leadership development for the youth of Oromia and Ethiopia at large.

Academic services and Scientific research

ODA Special Boarding School is committed to academic excellence, innovation, and research-oriented education. The school focuses on nurturing scientific curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills among its students through both classroom and extracurricular activities. Currently, the school provides: Advanced Science and Technology Laboratories equipped for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science experiments. STEM-focused Learning Programs designed to develop future scientists, engineers, and innovators. Research and Innovation Clubs that encourage students to undertake mini research projects, science fairs, and exhibitions. Talent Development Programs aimed at identifying and supporting gifted students through mentorship and special academic guidance. Partnerships and Collaboration with universities, educational bureaus, and scientific institutions to enhance research experience and innovation culture. Digital Learning Platforms that integrate modern ICT tools to promote interactive and independent learning. Through these academic services, ODA Boarding School strives to become a center of research and excellence in science, technology, and innovation, contributing to the intellectual and socio-economic growth of Oromia and Ethiopia at large.
